# User Manual

## Dashboard
The dashboard gives you a quick summary of your business:
- **Total Invoices**: Number of invoices generated.
- **Total Revenue**: Sum of all invoices.
- **Clients & Products**: Counts of your database entries.
- **Recent Invoices**: A list of the 5 most recent invoices.

## Managing Clients
1.  Navigate to the **Clients** tab.
2.  Click **Add Client** to create a new customer.
3.  Fill in the details (Name, Email, Phone, Address, NIT).
4.  Click **Save**.
5.  You can Edit or Delete clients from the list.

## Managing Products
1.  Navigate to the **Products** tab.
2.  Click **Add Product**.
3.  Enter Name, Description, Price, and Stock.
4.  Click **Save**.

## Creating an Invoice
1.  Navigate to **Invoices**.
2.  Click **Create Invoice**.
3.  **Select Client**: Choose a client from the dropdown.
4.  **Add Items**:
    - Select a product.
    - Adjust quantity.
    - Price is auto-filled but can be modified if needed (in future updates).
5.  **Tax**: Adjust the tax rate (default 19%).
6.  **Notes**: Add any payment instructions or notes.
7.  Click **Create Invoice**.

## Downloading PDF
1.  In the **Invoices** list, locate the invoice.
2.  Click the **Download** (Green Arrow) icon.
3.  The PDF will be generated and downloaded to your computer.
